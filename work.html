Directions to Use :

STEP 1 :

Click on the mic to Speak

STEP 2 :

Just speak your queries like -
# To calculate addition (+) ,
Like, 8+1, say: eight plus one.
# To calculate subtraction (-) ,
Like, 5-1, say five minus one.
# To calculate multiplication (*) ,
Like, 6*3 say: six multiplied by three.
# To calculate division (/) ,
Like, 7/3 say: seven divided by three.

Result :

Tap on equal to button to get the result.
Users can do many Simple and Scientific calculations.
